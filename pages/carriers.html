Carriers

Oscar Rodriguez

Today, in our contemporary lives, international tourism plays a significant role in our society, various academic disciplines have repeatedly sought to re-evaluate the significance of tourism. Its importance is evident international tourist arrivals reached 1,014 million in the first ten months in 2015. According to United Nation World Tourism Organisation (UNWTO) World Barometer between January and October 2015, 43 million more tourists (overnight visitors) travelled to international destinations around the world compared to the same period last year, corresponding to increase by 4%. International tourism is the most rapid growth market in the global economy.

In tourism studies, tourism is sometimes classified as an industry or as a set of organisations working in a network (system). The purpose of the present discussion is to find out the features that distinguish the tourism industry from tourism as a system. This study identifies these differences into the following three concepts; contemporary tourism system, discussion of tourism as a system, and the industrial tourism environment. Besides, the differences in these concepts will contribute to recognising the construction of international tourism.
